Apex Public School

Apex Public school was and is the brain child of Late Mr. A.M Jonathan, founder Principal; and Educationist established on July, 29, 1985. It is an unaided Christian minority institution run by the J.C.E Society registered Apex Public School 10+2, is recognized by the Directorate of Education and is affiliated to the C.B.S.E. It is co-educational and the mental, physical, moral and social growth of children is its main aim. This development is given direction by the PRINCIPAL AND STAFF It is the first day boarding school of Delhi in 4 acres of pollution free land…. Besides curricular activities, the games field and courts are used to inculcate team spirit, fraternity and physical well-being. “Education, to be complete, must be humane; it must include not only the training of intellect, but also the refinement of the heart and the discipline of the spirit”
